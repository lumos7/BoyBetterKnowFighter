Special
===============
Gou Hadouken : QCF + P

Shakunetsu Gou Hadouken : HCB + P

Gou Zankuu : QCF + P (in air)

Gou Shoryuken : dp + P

Tatsumaki Zankuu Kyakuu : QCB + K

Tenma Kujin Kyaku : QCF + K (in air)

                rev dp +2P / rev dp + 2K (backward)
*Punches go farther than kicks
